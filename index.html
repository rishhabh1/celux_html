Celer + Lux

Celer: in Latin means swiftness, rapidity of motion & Lux: in Latin means light

Combining Celer and Lux we formed the word “Celux” meaning moving at the speed of light. We believe moving rapidly leads to business success.

Why?

Given the exponential advancement in technology over past decades and even more over the foreseeable future, rapidly advancing technology and advanced processes will benefit people and thus every organisation embracing innovation. Celux aims to enable organisations to rapidly embrace and embed impending changes in technology and business processes.
